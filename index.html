Information

Registration for the Spring 2013 Commencement Ceremony begins February 1, 2013 and will be open through April 12, 2013.

Any student who has completed the 160 units required, and who has not already participated in a past undergraduate commencement ceremony, is eligible and welcome to participate in the Spring 2013 Commencement ceremony.

Any student who expects to receive a degree in September or December is encouraged to participate in the Fall 2013 commencement which is held closer to the completion of your studies. Typically the Fall is a smaller ceremony and allows for students to invite more guests. The FALL CEREMONY will be on Saturday, December 14th 2013.
